About Us

Eduscope is an online career pathway finder that iterates over different job vacancies on the website to provide the user with the best match job based on their qualifications.

Our Values


PURPOSE: To enable users to fully examine career options and to help them make career decisions. Give advice required and information on options across a variety of factors such as; previous earnings and jobs, potential routes to obtain necessary qualifications, training involved and other opportunities that may arise.


AIMS: To make the process of finding a career easy without requiring a knowledge in computer literate. To provide a secure system that is trustworthy and reliable.

OBJECTIVES: Make a user-friendly website. Provide a good visual appeal along with the content. Rich sector knowledge to make a faster search time and accurate results.
